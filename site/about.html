About

My name is Jeffrey Wang, and I love to explore the city on my bike. Whether it's navigating bustling South End or exploring the artsy shops of NoDa, my bike lets me experience this vibrant city with each pedal stroke. As a first-year computer science and music major, I love both the logical order of code and the expressive freedom of music alike--a duality reflected in Charlotte's diverse neighborhoods. Join me on this journey, virtually or on the streets. I'm always up to discover the next hidden corner, coffee shop, or concert that Charlotte has to offer!
